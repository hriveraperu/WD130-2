Overview

Purpose

We are sure you noticed that almost everything is "Made in China". You did ask yourself questions like: How can I contact them? How can I start to import all those products by myself? Difficult task, but we can make it possible! We can help you make contact with different Chinese factories for different cities and industries, you don't need to learn Chinese or negotiate with them, we already do this successfully with many of my customers and help them import what their customers need.

Audience

Our intended audience are people from any age, any gender, any country except Asia, preferably businesspeople, store owners, who already have a market and experience with certain products they sell in their respective websites or stores, but they purchase all their products locally to a provider or importer and wants to improve their profitability doing import business from China, and they speak Spanish, English but do not speak Chinese. The audience must identify their needs on the website.

Expertise

Experience We have more than 10 years of experience in the import/export market, we traveled to China, Japan, Taiwan and attended different fairs, company offices and in-place factories. Our knowledge about the different factory areas allow us to find your product in the right place, in the right price. How to-do?

Requirement First, we recieve the requirement, specifying the needs of the product or products and the description. Then we contact our database of providers to help us with the quotations, we give references if needed. Proforma After we recieve the information, we give it to you as a proforma, including the price and our comission that varies according to the product and procedence. Then we await your analyze and counter-offer, to set the best price for you and our company. If needed also we can give a door-to-door quotation. Logistics After the confirmation of the proforma, we start the process from the provider, insurance, customs to export, shipping company, customs to import, and local freight, if you are not familiarized with the import business. Deliver After the products arrived and are cleared by customs, they are delivered to your warehouse.
